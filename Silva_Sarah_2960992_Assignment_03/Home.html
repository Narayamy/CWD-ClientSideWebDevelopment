An important Hero

Humans normally think they are the best species in the world, but if we think about it we might see that it is not too hard once you have arms, legs, fingers and off course a powerful brain capable of extraordinary things.

We Humans are undeniable smart and a thinkable specie, but have we ever considered whether we would think this way if we did not have arms or legs or fingers and still be able to do all the significant activities to survive?

Snakes, on the other hand, do not possess any of those cited before and still they are able of mostly everything. These animals have to struggle through life to survive against predators; they have to hunt their praise. They are legless reptiles commonly misunderstood as a dangerous animal. Even though their venom (not all of them) can be dangerous and actually kill someone, these animals only bite when threatened.

Rattlesnakes in particular own a rattle at the end of their tail which makes a loud rattling noise when vibrated to stop predators or serves as a warning to passers-by. Among other characteristics this is what makes this animal unique specie on nature.
