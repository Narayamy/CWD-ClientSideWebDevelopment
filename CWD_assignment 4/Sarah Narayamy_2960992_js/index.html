Question 1

St. Patrick's day

St. Patrick is known for bring days of good times and green beer to pubs across the world.
From the sentences below, choose the incorrect one.


St. Patrick wasn’t made a saint until centuries after his death
St. Patrick was born in Britain to a wealthy family
During his childhood, he was kidnapped and sold into slavery in Ireland
St. Patrick taught the Irish about the Christian religion
St. Patrick became a hero as soon as he died
There never were any snakes in Ireland

Incorrect answer.

Correct answer!

St. Patrick was soon forgotten after his death. It wasn’t until many years later that monks began telling the tale of St. Patrick forcing all the snakes out of Ireland.

The Irish Language

Gaeltacht is an Irish-language word used to denote any primarily Irish-speaking region. In Ireland, the term Gaeltacht refers individually to any, or collectively to all, of the districts where the government recognises that the Irish language is the predominant vernacular, or language of the home.
